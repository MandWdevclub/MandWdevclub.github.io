About Us

Our club consists of students who truly care about the creation of new ideas through the use of software development. We facilitate this by teaching each other through workshops and investing our time in the Virginia Tech student developer community. We're an open source community, and to us, that means that we accept everyone willing to learn or give back through teaching.
